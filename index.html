About

I am an undergraduate student at Seoul National University double majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering and Economics, and an incoming D.D.S. student at the School of Dentistry, Seoul National University. My academic training emphasizes analytical thinking, data-driven reasoning, and evidence-based decision making.

My research experience includes biomedical data analysis, where I developed a deep learning pipeline to enhance detection of glioma cell-free DNA from RRBS methylation data, as well as the development of a digital oral health management application for elderly users in collaboration with the School of Dentistry.

I am broadly interested in how technology, data, and systems thinking can be applied to patient-centered, preventive, and public health–oriented dentistry.
