About

I am a D.D.S. student at the School of Dentistry, Seoul National University. My academic training emphasizes analytical thinking, data-driven reasoning, and evidence-based decision making, which I aim to apply to the field of dentistry.

My research experience spans from how to make reliable data systems, data mining, differences in convergence of iterations based on initial conditions in recommendation systems to biomedical data analysis, digital health technologies, and preventive dental research. I am particularly interested in how quantitative analysis and system-level thinking can be translated into preventive strategies and equitable healthcare delivery in dentistry.
